New York Type Book

From the Statue of Liberty to Times Square, New York is an American icon. The art scene in New York City is bold, exciting and diverse. The City is known for its great influence on Pop Art, Street Art and Abstract Expressionism. Additionally, it houses many of America's first art galleries. The streets are packed with creative, fashionable and unique people, including talented typographers. This book features 10 of some of the most renowned typographers based in New York. Though knowing the state, there are definetly dozens of more unique typographers that could ve been in nis book.
